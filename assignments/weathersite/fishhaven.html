Fun Fish Haven Facts

Information found on Wikipedia

Did you know that Fish Haven at one point in time was originally named Rush Creek? Fish Haven was settled way back in 1864. It was named Fish Haven because Bear Lake, right next door, is a favorite fishing spot. Apperently, a post office was established in 1862, and remained until 1967. That's over 100 years.
